Resort Activities

Unforgettable Experiences

Discover a world of adventure and relaxation with our wide range of activities. Whether you seek thrills or tranquility, we have something for everyone.

Scuba Diving

Explore the vibrant underwater world with our professional diving instructors. Discover colorful coral reefs, exotic fish, and marine life in crystal-clear waters.

Duration 3-4 hours
Difficulty Moderate
Group Size Max 8 people

What to Bring

  • Swimwear
  • Towel
  • Sunscreen
  • Waterproof camera (optional)

Best Time

Morning dives (8-11 AM) offer the best visibility and marine life activity.

Spa & Wellness

Indulge in a range of rejuvenating treatments at our luxury spa. From massages to facials, our skilled therapists will help you relax and unwind.

Duration 1-2 hours
Difficulty Easy
Session Type Individual

Available Treatments

  • Swedish Massage
  • Deep Tissue Massage
  • Hot Stone Therapy
  • Facial Treatments
  • Body Wraps

Best Time

Appointments available daily from 9 AM to 8 PM. Evening sessions (after 5 PM) are most popular.

Hiking Adventure

Embark on a guided hiking tour through lush forests and scenic trails. Enjoy breathtaking views and discover the local flora and fauna.

Duration 4-5 hours
Difficulty Moderate to Hard
Group Size Max 12 people

What to Bring

  • Comfortable hiking shoes
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Hat and sunglasses
  • Water bottle (we provide refills)
  • Camera

Trail Options

  • Sunrise Peak (Moderate) - 3 hours
  • Waterfall Trail (Easy) - 2 hours
  • Mountain Summit (Hard) - 5 hours

Cooking Class

Learn to prepare local cuisine with our expert chefs. Discover traditional recipes and techniques, then enjoy the delicious meal you've created.

Duration 3 hours
Difficulty Easy
Group Size Max 12 people

Water Sports

Get your adrenaline pumping with a variety of water sports including jet skiing, parasailing, and paddleboarding. Fun for all ages and skill levels.

Duration Varies by activity
Difficulty Easy to Moderate
Age Requirement 12+ (varies by activity)

Available Activities

  • Jet Skiing (30 min sessions)
